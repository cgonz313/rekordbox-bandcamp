Bandcamp to rekordbox playlist converter

1 · Local Library

Add your music folder(s) and click Index Library. The first scan may take a few minutes — future scans use a cache and are much faster.

2 · Bandcamp

Opens a browser window to log into Bandcamp. Your credentials are never stored — once logged in, your playlists load here automatically.

3 · Playlists

Select playlists, choose a save location, then click Export. Import the XML into rekordbox via Preferences → Advanced → rekordbox xml.
