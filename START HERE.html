What is this?

Converts your private Bandcamp playlists into a rekordbox XML file, matched against your local music library.

  • 1Opens a browser so you can log into Bandcamp normally
  • 2Fuzzy-matches each playlist track to your local files using their metadata tags
  • 3Generates an XML file you import directly into rekordbox

🔒 Your Bandcamp credentials are never stored — the browser session works just like a normal login. Everything runs locally on your machine.
